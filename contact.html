FAQ

Common Questions

What hardware does CRYO-CORE support?
CRYO-CORE currently supports standard 120mm and 240mm radiator mounting points across AMD AM4/AM5 and Intel LGA 1700/1200 sockets. Compatibility lists are updated with each firmware release via the ADAPT software dashboard.
Does Submerge integrate with our DCIM?
Yes. Submerge exposes a REST API and SNMP traps compatible with leading DCIM platforms including Sunbird, Nlyte, and Schneider EcoStruxure. Custom integrations are available under enterprise agreements.
How is heat-spike prediction trained?
Our ML models are trained on a combination of synthetic thermal workload traces and anonymized telemetry from opted-in deployments. Each model update is validated against held-out workload profiles before OTA delivery to ensure no regression in prediction accuracy.
What is the warranty?
CRYO-CORE hardware carries a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. Submerge enterprise systems ship with a 3-year on-site service agreement. Extended warranty options are available through authorized resellers.
Can I trial a unit?
Qualified enterprise and OEM partners can request a 30-day evaluation unit through the contact form above. Consumer pre-orders will open when we launch the CRYO-CORE retail channel — sign up via the contact form to be notified first.
How do I become a partner?
Select "Partnerships" in the topic dropdown on the contact form and describe your use case. Our business development team typically responds within 2 business days. We welcome OEM embedding, distribution, and co-development conversations.
