Predictive AIO

Core Technology

Three pillars of the LIQUID ecosystem

speed

Predictive Pump Control

Onboard sensors feed real-time telemetry into a self-learning ML model that predicts heat spikes seconds before they occur. Pump speed and coolant flow adjust proactively — eliminating reactive throttling and preserving sustained boost clocks.

lock

Sealed Loop Reliability

The fully sealed, pre-filled coolant loop is pressure-tested and rated for zero maintenance over its 5-year lifespan. Corrosion-inhibiting coolant formulation protects the pump, radiator, and CPU cold plate across the entire thermal envelope.

palette

ARGB & Companion App

ARGB Gen 2 header syncs pump-head and fan illumination with all major RGB ecosystems. The Windows companion app — with macOS in preview — surfaces thermal history, pump curves, and firmware updates in a single dashboard.

  • sensors
    Multi-Point Telemetry Embedded sensors sample coolant inlet and outlet temperatures, pump speed, fan RPM, CPU package power, and ambient conditions at 10 ms intervals. This dense telemetry stream is the foundation the ML model uses to distinguish gaming bursts, render workloads, and idle states — predicting heat spikes with high confidence before they manifest.
  • speed
    Predictive Pump Speed Adjustments Rather than reacting to rising temperatures, the onboard ML model projects thermal demand seconds ahead and pre-emptively ramps pump speed and coolant flow. The result is consistently cooler CPU die temperatures and sustained boost clocks without the acoustic penalty of full-speed fan spin-up.
  • auto_awesome
    Self-Learning Optimisation After each session the model compares its thermal predictions against actual sensor outcomes and updates its internal weights. Over days of use, LIQUID calibrates its pump and fan curves to your specific chip, case airflow, and usage patterns — becoming progressively quieter, more efficient, and more accurate without any manual tuning.
