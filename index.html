How It Works

  1. 01

    Sense

    Distributed sensor arrays capture temperature, flow rate, ambient conditions, and workload signals every 50 ms.

  2. 02

    Predict

    On-device ML models project thermal trajectories 2–5 seconds ahead, flagging spikes before they reach the die.

  3. 03

    Adapt

    The controller adjusts pump speed, coolant flow, and fan curves in real time to intercept the predicted thermal event.

  4. 04

    Learn

    Each cycle refines the model. The system continuously improves efficiency and accuracy over weeks and months of operation.
